Revised key steps for the introduction section of an academic paper on machine learning:

1. Clearly state the problem you're addressing and the specific research questions or objectives.
2. Provide a clear motivation for the proposed model, explaining its novelty and potential impact, and establish the research's importance and relevance to the AI community. 
3. Provide a comprehensive overview of related work, highlighting the main differences from your work and explaining how your work builds upon and extends the existing literature.
4. Clearly state the main contribution of the paper and the proposed model, including its unique features and technical contributions.
5. Provide a structured approach and technical details of the proposed model, highlighting its advantages over existing models.
6. Conclude the introduction by summarizing the significance of the research and its potential impact on the field of machine learning.
