Revised key steps for the introduction section of an academic paper on machine learning:

1. Clearly state the problem being addressed and its significance to the AI community.
2. Provide a comprehensive overview of related work, highlighting the gaps in the existing literature that your research aims to fill.
3. Clearly state the main contribution of the paper and the specific research questions or objectives.
4. Provide a clear motivation for the proposed research, explaining why the proposed model is necessary and how it addresses the gaps in the existing literature.
5. Explain the proposed model and techniques in detail, including the technical details and experimental results to support the effectiveness of the proposed model.
6. Provide a clear comparison with previous work and explain the methodology used, highlighting the advantages of the proposed method over existing methods. Conclude the introduction with a summary of the key contributions and a clear roadmap for the rest of the paper. Use a clear and concise writing style throughout.
