Revised key steps for writing an introduction section of a machine learning paper:

1. Clearly state the research question or objective of the paper, highlighting its significance and potential impact.
2. Provide a comprehensive overview of related works in the field, highlighting their strengths and weaknesses and how they relate to the proposed approach. Provide specific references to related work.
3. Explain the importance of addressing the problem and provide a clear motivation for the proposed model or approach. 
4. Provide a clear and concise summary of the proposed approach, highlighting its novelty and how it addresses the research question. 
5. Summarize the main contributions of the paper, highlighting the key findings and how they advance the field. 
6. Provide a clear and logical structure for the paper, outlining the main sections and their contents.
