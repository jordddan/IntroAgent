Revised key steps for the introduction section of an academic paper on machine learning:

1. Provide a clear motivation for the proposed model, highlighting the problem it aims to solve and its significance to the AI community.
2. Give a comprehensive overview of related work, highlighting the gaps in the literature that your research aims to fill.
3. Clearly state your research questions or hypotheses and the proposed solution or method, emphasizing the novelty and significance of your approach.
4. Describe the proposed model and its components, including the experimental setup, evaluation metrics, and limitations of existing models, to provide a clear understanding of your methodology.
5. Discuss the main contributions and significance of the paper, highlighting how the proposed method addresses the research questions and contributes to the field, and discussing potential future directions for research.
6. Clearly state the experimental results' significance and how they support the proposed method's effectiveness in addressing the research questions.
