Revised key steps for the introduction section of a machine learning paper:

1. Clearly state the research problem and its significance to the AI community.
2. Provide a comprehensive overview of related work and explain how your proposed approach differs from prior works.
3. Clearly state the main contribution of your research and provide a clear motivation for the proposed approach, highlighting the potential impact of the research.
4. Explain the technical details of the proposed approach and its novelty in detail, including any limitations or assumptions.
5. Provide a roadmap for the rest of the paper, including a brief summary of each section and its contribution to the research.
6. Summarize the key contributions of your research and highlight the potential implications for future research.
